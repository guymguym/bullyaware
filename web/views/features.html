Bullyaware features

Require teenager’s cooperation and agreement

Bullyaware is a tool that enables teens and parents to deal with cyberbullying using technology. Unlike parental control systems, it requires the teenager's agreement.


Analyze any social networks for cyberbullying risks

Cyberbullying takes many forms and shapes. It can include profanities, abusive language, humiliation, exclusion and more. Bullyaware identifies suspicious patterns and helps in early cyberbullying identification.


Provide information on best response to specific cyberbullying situation

Not all cyberbullying requires the same response. Good response may vary from ignoring it altogether, to reporting and engaging the authorities. Bullyaware will provide you with the various alternatives which best fit the situation.


Alert kids when they behave like bullies

Being a cyberbully has sever implications outside the internet and in real life for both the victim and the abuser. Not all cyberbullies are aware of the implications of their misconduct, which might expose both themselves and their parents to face legal charges. Bullyaware notifies oblivious abusers and helps reduce the risk on both victims and bullies.


Features you won't find on Bullyaware

Forcefully monitor unwillful teenager

Bullyaware is a strong believer in teenagers’ autonomy. If a teenager does not see the value in the service we provide or requests us to stop monitor his/her account, we will always respond.


The perfect spying tool

If a person is aware that he is being monitored, there is always a way to escapte the monitoring. As that is the case, we do not provide the perfect spying tool, but a solution that makes sense for both the parents as well as the teenager.


Become the perfect punishment tool

Parents must not punish or judge teenagers due to alerts received on Bullyaware. If we will become aware of such misconduct by a friend or other source, we will stop our interaction.
